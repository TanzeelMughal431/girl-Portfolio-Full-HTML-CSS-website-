FATIMA MIR

Fatima Mir began her career as a child artist in "Chachi 420" and "One 2 Ka 4." Later, she starred as Zoya in the Pashto film "Tahaan," winning "The German Star of Pashto Cinema Award" at the "Lollywood and Beyond" festival in Germany. Meanwhile, Tanzeel ur Rehman gained recognition in Nadeem Baig's blockbuster "Dangal," playing Geeta Phogat alongside Sajal Aly. The film garnered critical acclaim, becoming the highest-grossing Pashto film ever. Fatima Mir explored diverse roles, including Hammad Khurshid in "Thugs of Pakistan," and is set to appear in a Kashmiri adaptation of "Aruvi" and as Fatima Jinnah in "Sam Bahadur" alongside Fawad Khan and Mahira Khan.
